About us

As students, we understand how challenging it can be to solve problems involving physics that demand analytical skills. Focusing on one topic, kinematics, we were inspired to have this site in an attempt to make the topic less intimidating and to encourage learning. Our all-in-one kinematics solver ought to bridge the gap left by existing sites and provide a more complete and convenient experience.

We are Karyl Mangosan, Kate Manzano, Nicole Mosquite, and Angela Tabarangao, first-year Chemical Engineering students at Bicol University and the founders of Kinemania. This site was built to help fellow students better understand and solve motion-related physics problems more easily. This project mirrors our passion for learning and our goal to apply what we learn in practical problems.
